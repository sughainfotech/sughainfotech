Managed Cloud & Infrastructure Services

Sughainfotech provides focused managed cloud and infrastructure support for small and mid-size businesses using Azure and AWS.

Our goal is simple: to keep your systems stable, secure, and cost-efficient — without adding complexity or full-time hiring costs.


Azure & AWS Cloud Management

We manage day-to-day cloud operations, including configuration reviews, resource usage tracking, and best-practice alignment to ensure your cloud environment runs smoothly.

Servers & Virtual Machines

We monitor and maintain servers and virtual machines to prevent performance issues, failures, and misconfigurations before they impact your business.

Backup & Disaster Recovery

We review and manage backup strategies to ensure your data is protected and recoverable in case of accidental deletion, system failure, or outages.

Monitoring & Alerts

We help set up and review monitoring and alerting mechanisms so potential issues are identified early and addressed before they become serious problems.

Patch & Security Management

We assist with regular patching and basic security hygiene to reduce vulnerabilities and improve system stability without disrupting daily operations.

Cloud Cost Optimization

We analyze cloud usage and identify unnecessary spend, helping you reduce costs gradually while maintaining required performance and reliability.
